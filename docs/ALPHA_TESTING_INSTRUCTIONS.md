## ğŸ’¡ Testing Tips

### Get the Most Value
- **Try everything**: Don't just stick to one feature
- **Test like you'd actually use it**: What would make you book a real class?
- **Break things**: Try edge cases, weird inputs, rapid tapping
- **Network conditions**: Test on WiFi and cellular

### Common Issues to Watch For
- **Slow loading**: How long do screens take to appear?
- **Authentication**: Do all login methods work reliably?
- **Payment security**: Does the payment flow feel trustworthy?
- **Class information**: Is there enough detail to make booking decisions?
- **Error handling**: What happens when things go wrong?

### Vancouver-Specific Testing
- **Local studios**: Do you recognize any of the studios?
- **Accurate info**: Are locations and descriptions correct?
- **Missing studios**: What creative spaces should we add?
- **Pricing**: Do credit costs feel reasonable for Vancouver?
