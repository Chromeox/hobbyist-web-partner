# Non-Disclosure Agreement - Business Partners/Co-Founders

**Date:** _______________  
**Between:** HobbyApp Inc. and _____________________

---

## 1. Confidential Information

The following information is considered confidential and proprietary:

### Business Strategy:
- Platform Profitability Model and financial projections
- User acquisition costs and revenue per user data
- Partnership strategies and studio acquisition plans
- Marketing strategies and competitive positioning

### Technical Information:
- App architecture and development roadmap
- Database schemas and API structures
- Partner portal functionality and features
- User behavior analytics and platform metrics

### Financial Information:
- Revenue models and commission structures
- Funding requirements and investment plans
- Operational costs and profit margins
- Studio payout calculations and financial flows

---

## 2. Purpose and Permitted Use

Confidential information is shared solely for:
- Evaluating potential business partnership opportunities
- Discussing advisory roles or co-founder arrangements
- Assessing investment or collaboration possibilities

**Information may NOT be used for:**
- Developing competing platforms or services
- Soliciting HobbyApp's studio partners or users
- Any purpose other than the stated evaluation

---

## 3. Non-Disclosure and Non-Use

**Receiving party agrees to:**
- Maintain strict confidentiality of all shared information
- Not disclose information to any third parties without written consent
- Use information solely for evaluation purposes
- Implement reasonable security measures to protect information
- Not reverse engineer or attempt to replicate any technical solutions

---

## 4. Non-Compete and Non-Solicitation

For **18 months** from the date of this agreement:

### Non-Compete:
- Will not develop, fund, or participate in competing hobby/creative class platforms
- Will not target the Vancouver creative class market with similar services
- Will not use confidential information to benefit competing businesses

### Non-Solicitation:
- Will not recruit or solicit HobbyApp employees or contractors
- Will not approach HobbyApp's studio partners for competing services
- Will not target HobbyApp's user base for competing platforms

---

## 5. Return of Materials

Upon termination of discussions or upon request:
- All confidential documents must be returned or securely destroyed
- Digital copies must be permanently deleted from all devices
- Written confirmation of destruction/deletion must be provided

---

## 6. Remedies and Enforcement

**Monetary Damages:** Breach may cause irreparable harm exceeding monetary damages

**Injunctive Relief:** HobbyApp may seek immediate injunctive relief for breaches

**Legal Costs:** Breaching party responsible for all legal fees and costs

---

## 7. Term and Survival

- **Agreement Duration:** 3 years from signing
- **Confidentiality Period:** 5 years from disclosure of specific information
- **Non-Compete Period:** 18 months from signing
- **Survival:** All obligations survive termination of discussions

---

## 8. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of British Columbia, Canada.
