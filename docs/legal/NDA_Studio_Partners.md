# Non-Disclosure Agreement - Studio Partners

**Date:** _______________  
**Between:** HobbyApp Inc. and _____________________ Studio

---

## 1. Confidential Information

Both parties agree to keep confidential any business information shared during discussions including:

### HobbyApp Confidential Information:
- Commission rates and revenue sharing models
- Platform technology and business strategies
- User acquisition strategies and marketing plans
- Financial projections and partnership terms
- Partner portal functionality and features

### Studio Confidential Information:
- Class pricing and revenue information
- Student lists and booking data
- Business operations and strategies
- Financial information and growth plans

---

## 2. Non-Disclosure Obligations

**Both parties agree to:**
- Keep all shared information strictly confidential
- Not share information with third parties without written consent
- Use information only for evaluating potential partnership
- Return or delete confidential materials upon request

---

## 3. Non-Compete (Vancouver Market)

For a period of **12 months** from signing, neither party will:
- Develop competing hobby/creative class booking platforms in Vancouver
- Poach the other party's studio partners or key personnel
- Use confidential information to compete directly

---

## 4. Term and Termination

- **Duration:** 2 years from signing date
- **Termination:** Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- **Survival:** Confidentiality obligations continue for 3 years after termination

---

## 5. Exclusions

This agreement does not cover information that:
- Is publicly available or becomes public through no breach
- Was known prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of confidential information
