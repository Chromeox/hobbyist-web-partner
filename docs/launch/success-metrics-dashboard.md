## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Glossary

### **User Metrics**

**Active Users**
- Definition: Users who opened app in past 7 days
- Target: 70% of total registered users
- Why it matters: Indicates engagement and stickiness

**Day X Retention**
- Definition: % of users who return X days after signup
- Targets: D1 >80%, D7 >70%, D30 >50%
- Why it matters: Predicts long-term viability

**Sign-Up Completion Rate**
- Definition: (Completed sign-ups / Started sign-ups) √ó 100
- Target: >95%
- Why it matters: Measures auth flow friction

**Onboarding Completion Rate**
- Definition: (Completed 6 steps / Started onboarding) √ó 100
- Target: >90%
- Why it matters: Measures first-time user experience

**Credit Utilization Rate**
- Definition: (Credits spent / Credits loaded) √ó 100
- Target: 70-75%
- Why it matters: Shows actual engagement with platform value

---

### **Booking Metrics**

**Bookings per User**
- Definition: Total bookings / Total users
- Target: >2.0 in first 30 days
- Why it matters: Shows repeat usage behavior

**Booking Completion Rate**
- Definition: (Bookings completed / Bookings started) √ó 100
- Target: >60%
- Why it matters: Measures checkout flow friction

**Avg Booking Value**
- Definition: Total GMV / Total bookings
- Target: $20-30 (typical intro class price)
- Why it matters: Revenue per transaction

---

### **Studio Metrics**

**Active Studios**
- Definition: Studios with ‚â•1 booking in past 30 days
- Target: >80% of total studios
- Why it matters: Partner platform health

**Classes per Studio**
- Definition: Total classes listed / Total studios
- Target: 5-10 classes per studio
- Why it matters: Catalog depth and variety

**Avg Bookings per Studio**
- Definition: Total bookings / Total studios
- Target: 20-50 per month
- Why it matters: Studio satisfaction and revenue

**Studio Churn Rate**
- Definition: (Studios who left / Total studios) √ó 100 per month
- Target: <10%
- Why it matters: Partner retention health

---

### **Revenue Metrics**

**GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)**
- Definition: Total value of all bookings
- Formula: Sum of all booking amounts
- Why it matters: Market size indicator

**Platform Revenue**
- Definition: 30% commission on GMV
- Formula: GMV √ó 0.30
- Why it matters: Your actual revenue

**CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**
- Definition: Total marketing spend / New users acquired
- Target: <$20
- Why it matters: Profitability of growth

**LTV (Lifetime Value)**
- Definition: Avg revenue per user over lifetime
- Target: >$100 (60-day proxy)
- Why it matters: How much each user is worth

**LTV:CAC Ratio**
- Definition: LTV √∑ CAC
- Target: >5:1 (healthy), >3:1 (acceptable)
- Why it matters: Growth sustainability

**Payback Period**
- Definition: Months to recover CAC from user revenue
- Target: <6 months
- Why it matters: Cash flow and runway

---

### **Product Health Metrics**

**Crash Rate**
- Definition: (Sessions with crash / Total sessions) √ó 100
- Target: <1%
- Why it matters: Product stability

**App Rating**
- Definition: Avg star rating from users
- Target: >4.0
- Why it matters: User satisfaction and App Store ranking

**Bug Bounce Count**
- Definition: Critical bugs reported per week
- Target: <5 new, <3 unfixed
- Why it matters: Product quality trajectory

## Red Flag Response Playbook

### If Crash Rate >3%
1. Halt new user onboarding immediately
2. Review Firebase/Sentry crash logs
3. Identify top 3 crash causes
4. Fix within 24-48 hours
5. Deploy hotfix via TestFlight
6. Resume onboarding only when <1%

### If Retention <50% by Day 7
1. Exit interviews with churned users (ask why)
2. Analyze drop-off points in funnel
3. Hypothesis: Onboarding too complex? Not enough classes? Booking friction?
4. A/B test fixes (simplified onboarding, better discovery, etc.)
5. Re-measure after 1 week

### If Zero Bookings After 3 Days
1. Test booking flow yourself end-to-end
2. Check Stripe integration (test payment)
3. Ask alpha testers directly: "Why haven't you booked?"
4. Look for UX blockers (confusing UI, hidden classes, etc.)
5. Fix immediately‚Äîbookings are the whole point

### If Studios Complaining
1. Call studio owner immediately (don't email)
2. Listen to complaint fully without defending
3. Offer immediate solution or workaround
4. Fix underlying issue within 48 hours
5. Follow up to confirm satisfaction
6. Learn: What systemic issue caused this?

## Final Thoughts on Metrics

### **Don't Vanity Metric Chase**
- Downloads ‚â† Active Users
- Sign-ups ‚â† Bookings
- Classes Listed ‚â† Classes Booked
- Focus on engagement and revenue, not surface metrics

### **Context Matters**
- 25 active users in Week 1 is amazing
- 25 active users in Week 8 is concerning
- Compare to your targets, not other startups

### **Metrics Inform, Don't Dictate**
- Numbers tell you WHAT is happening
- Talk to users/studios to understand WHY
- Quantitative + Qualitative = Full picture

### **One Metric That Matters (OMTM)**
Each phase has one metric to obsess over:
- Week 1-3: Day 7 Retention (are users coming back?)
- Week 4-6: Bookings per User (are users engaging?)
- Week 7-8: LTV:CAC Ratio (is growth profitable?)

**Pick your OMTM, then move everything else to support it.**
