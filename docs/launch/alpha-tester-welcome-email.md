**Pro Tips for Sending Emails:**

1. **Personalize**: Use first names, reference their specific activity
2. **Keep it short**: Attention spans are short - get to the point
3. **Clear CTA**: Every email should have ONE clear next step
4. **Mobile-friendly**: Most will read on phone - short paragraphs
5. **Timing**: Send Tue-Thu 10am-2pm (best open rates)
6. **Follow-up**: If no response in 3 days, gentle nudge
7. **Celebrate wins**: Recognize their contributions
8. **Be human**: You're a founder, not a corporation
