## ğŸ“ˆ Revenue Optimization Strategies

### 1. Psychological Pricing Tactics

**Anchoring**: Start with Unlimited at $199 to make $79 seem reasonable

**Decoy Effect**: Make Tier 2 ($49) intentionally less attractive than Tier 3 ($89)
- 15 credits at $3.27 each
- 30 credits at $2.97 each (only $0.30 difference!)
- Users feel smart choosing "better value"

**Urgency**: Limited-time offers
- "First month 50% off subscriptions"
- "Buy 30 credits, get 5 free (this week only)"
- "Flash sale: All packages 20% off"

### 2. Retention Strategies

**Credit Expiration**: 
- Packages: 6 months (encourages regular use)
- Subscriptions: Monthly with limited rollover

**Loyalty Rewards**:
- 10 classes = 1 free credit
- 25 classes = 3 free credits
- 50 classes = Premium upgrade for 1 month

**Referral Program**:
- Give 5 credits, Get 5 credits
- 3 successful referrals = 1 month free subscription

### 3. Conversion Optimization

**New User Journey**:
1. First class free (no credit card)
2. Offer: 5 credits for $9.99 (one-time)
3. Upsell: 15 credits for $39 (limited time)
4. Convert to subscription with 20% off first month

**Abandoned Cart Recovery**:
- 10% off if complete within 24h
- 15% off if complete within 48h
- Free bonus credits on next purchase
